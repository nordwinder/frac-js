Description
A simple JS program to draw a simplest type of fractals. Use:
Shift + Left Click on baseline segment to add node to baseline;
Ctrl (or Cmd) + Left Click on baseline node to remove node;
Left Click on node to move node;
Left Click on segment to reverse fractal on that segment;
Left Click on empty space to move whole figure.
